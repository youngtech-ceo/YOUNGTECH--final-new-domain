Young Tech Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026. This policy outlines how Young Tech gathers, secures, and handles your data when utilizing our forms, booking modals, and technical services.

1. Information Collection

We collect individual information explicitly provided by you when filling out our Project Request Forms, Dynamic Callback inputs, and Consultation booking loops. This data may encompass your full name, business email address, telephone contact number, estimated budget parameters, and direct product specification messages.

2. Use of Collected Data

Collected metadata is processed specifically to:

  • Evaluate your technological criteria and deliver customized project estimation blueprints.
  • Fulfill instant 10-minute callback sessions requested through the homepage callback tool.
  • Synchronize booking logs for consultation BRIEFING slots scheduled in our scheduler card.
  • Distribute periodic technology strategies and updates via email to newsletter subscribers.

3. Database Protection & Storage

We operate a highly secure server and database architecture backed by AWS and Firebase security parameters. Client inquiries, scheduler entries, and subscription logs are stored safely within Firebase Realtime Database clusters under strict data-access restrictions to block unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification.

4. Sharing of Information

Young Tech guarantees that we do not lease, trade, sell, or disclose your telephone numbers, billing information, or business details to third-party advertising companies. Data is accessed solely by our core engineering staff to support direct service provisions.

5. Privacy Concerns & Inquiries

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy or wish to request the manual deletion of your contact records from our database dashboard, please contact us directly at youngtechnology.gobi@gmail.com.
